Zero-Affiliation Policy

Formal declaration regarding the isolation of UXIDONE architecture and the prohibition of unauthorized external entities.

Scope

Core Mandate

This policy establishes the definitive boundaries of the UXIDONE SYSTEMS ecosystem and the UXIDONE root architecture. It serves as a public record that UXIDONE operates as a closed architectural sovereign, independent of unvetted third-party influencers, products, or individuals.

We operate with total architectural independence. This ensures that every line of code, design kernel, and system protocol is maintained within our secure, monitored perimeter without external interference.

Affiliation Restrictions

Strict Non-Association

UXIDONE does not maintain, recognize, or authorize any "affiliate" programs, referral networks, or partner-led distributions unless explicitly signed, sealed, and published via our primary digital nodes.

Unauthorized Product Prohibition

Any product, software, or digital asset not directly bearing the authenticated signature of UXIDONE SYSTEMS or originating from uxidone.github.io is strictly forbidden from claiming any technical, financial, or strategic association with our brand.

Verification Architecture

Authenticated Registry

To prevent deception, UXIDONE maintains a single source of truth for all authorized assets. If an entity is not listed within our authenticated registry, it is deemed non-affiliated.

Prohibited Activities

Enforcement Criteria

The following actions are classified as severe breaches of this policy:

  • The use of "UXIDONE" or "UXIDONE SYSTEMS" in marketing materials for unapproved products.
  • Misrepresentation of professional or individual ties to the UXIDONE internal team.
  • Distribution of derivative works that imply an official partnership where none exists.
  • Attempting to wrap UXIDONE logic within third-party "shells" without explicit licensing.
  • Claiming "Compatibility" or "Integration" status without a verified technical audit from our core team.

Violation Reporting

Integrity Maintenance

We actively monitor global repositories and social platforms for brand impersonation. If you encounter a product or individual falsely claiming affiliation with UXIDONE SYSTEMS or the UXIDONE brand, please report it immediately to our security node.

Reports are treated with total confidentiality and assist in the rapid deployment of legal cease-and-desist protocols.

Legal Recourse

System Defense

UXIDONE reserves the right to deploy automated technical countermeasures and pursue high-court legal proceedings against any entity that violates this isolation protocol. We maintain a policy of zero-tolerance regarding brand dilution and architectural contamination.
